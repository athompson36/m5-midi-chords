QUICK START
===============================================================

DE
1. Python installieren: https://www.python.org/downloads/
2. M5Stack CoreS3 per USB-C Datenkabel verbinden (Datenleitung, nicht nur Laden).
3. Windows: flash_firmware.bat doppelklicken.
4. macOS/Linux: python3 flash_firmware.py ausfuehren.

EN
1. Install Python 3: https://www.python.org/downloads/
2. Connect M5Stack CoreS3 with a USB-C cable that supports data.
3. Windows: double-click flash_firmware.bat.
4. macOS/Linux: run python3 flash_firmware.py.

===============================================================
AFTER FLASH: EXPECTED BEHAVIOR (CoreS3 UI)

1. Boot splash: "Hi! Let's Play" button appears on a blue background.
2. Tap the button to enter the play surface.
3. Play surface:
   - Top center shows the selected key (e.g. "C"). Tap to cycle keys.
   - Center square = key (tap to cycle). Eight square chord pads in a ring (3x3 minus center):
     I, ii, iii, IV, V, vi, vii, bVII — color-coded:
     GREEN = principal (I, IV, V)
     BLUE  = standard (ii, iii, vi)
     RED   = tension (vii dim)
     PINK  = borrowed (bVII)
   - Tap a chord to "play" it.
4. After 5 chord taps the key area shows a heart icon.
   Tap the heart for a surprise chord (modal interchange, etc.).
5. Settings: place two fingers on the left and right edges and hold ~0.8 s.
   BACK/FWD = move row, SELECT = change value, last row = Save & exit.
6. Adjustable: MIDI out channel, MIDI in (OMNI or channel), brightness, velocity.

===============================================================
IF SOMETHING FAILS

- Port not found:
  Try download mode: hold RESET for ~2 s until green LED, or try another cable.
- Flashing fails:
  Run python flash_firmware.py manually, or use FLASH_INSTRUCTIONS.md.
- Still blocked:
  Open FLASH_INSTRUCTIONS.md for full steps.
